Hosea Razanadrakoto

I'm a passionate mobile photography enthusiast, drawn to the beauty of simple, authentic moments. I like to add a cinematic touch to my images, playing with light, color, and framing to transform everyday life into scenes that tell a story.

Nature, landscapes, and everyday scenes are my main sources of inspiration. Each photo is an opportunity for me to capture an emotion, an atmosphere, or a detail that we sometimes forget to notice. Through this portfolio, I want to share my perspective and my sensitivity, and show that even with a simple smartphone, you can create powerful and evocative images.
